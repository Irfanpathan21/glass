DIY Ideas for Glass

Creative Ways to Reuse Glass

Glass Jars

Repurpose glass jars as stylish storage for spices, candles, or homemade jams. You can also paint or decorate them for use as vases, lanterns, or pen holders.

Glass Bottles

Transform glass bottles into decorative lights by adding fairy lights inside or turn them into unique flower vases. You can also use them for homemade infusions like oils or drinks.

Broken Glass Pieces

Use broken glass pieces to create mosaic art, coasters, or decorative mirrors. Ensure safety by smoothing the edges and securely gluing them to your base material.
